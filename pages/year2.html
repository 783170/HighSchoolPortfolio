I took this class during the 2020/2021 school year. This class was through the College Board, and consisted of a test at the end of the year in order to receive college credit. This test consisted of a 40 question multiple choice exam worth 50% of our score and a 4 question free response exam worth 50%.

This course includes a lab requirement of a minimum of 20 hours working as an individual or in a group. As well we were required to work in Java. This course had ten units: primitive types, objects, Boolean expressions, iteration, writing classes, arrays, ArrayList, 2D arrays, inheritance, and recursion.
