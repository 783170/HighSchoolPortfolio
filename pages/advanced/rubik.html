Changing the Reference Point

The up, down, left, and right arrows can be used to change the reference point of the cube marked by the black blinking square in the center of the face. The view of the cube will also be adjusted with the movement of the reference point.

to interact with the cube click on the cube's window and begin pressing keys

Changing the View

I created the cubes using P5.JS's WEBGL mode which creates 3D shapes.

To change the view of the cube move the cursor horizontally. To change the direction that the view rotates click the mouse, then continue to move the cursor horizontally. Changing the view does not change the reference point which will always be labelled by the black blinking square in the center of the face.

Turning the Cube's Faces

U' and U - use the T and Y keys to rotate the top face
L' and L - use the D and F keys to rotate the left face
F' and F - use the G and H keys to rotate the front face
R' and R - use the J and K keys to rotate the right face
B' and B - use the B and N keys to rotate the bottom face
