I took this class during the 2019/2020 school year. This class was through the College Board, and due to the COVID-19 outbreak the curriculum was changed. The multiple choice portion of the exam was cut, and 40% of our score was based on our Explore Performance Task and 60% was based on our Create Performance Task.

During The Explore Performance Task, we created a computational artifact and a written response. During this task we researched a computational innovation including:

  • Its impact on society, economy, or culture and explain how this impact could be beneficial and/or harmful
  • How it consumes, produces, or transforms data
  • Any data storage, data privacy, or data security concerns the innovation faces

And during the Create Performance Task, we created an app in a coding language of our choice (JavaScript) and included in our submission:

  • The complete program code
  • A video demonstrating our program in action
  • Where in the code we developed an algorithm that uses two other algorithms
  • An abstraction that maintains the complexity of the program
